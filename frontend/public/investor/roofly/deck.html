01The problem

Rental management in Malaysia still runs on chat threads.

Most landlords with 1–20 units operate without proper software. Their workflow is held together by WhatsApp, Excel, scattered PDFs, and memory-based reminders.

Operations live in five places at once.

Rental income, agreements, maintenance, and tenant comms each sit in a different app. Nothing reconciles.

Bank app Memory Sticky notes

Things slip through the cracks as portfolios grow.

Late rent goes uncaught. Agreements expire silently. Maintenance loops back to the landlord on Sunday night.

Lost receiptsTax season chaos
03 — tenant friction

Tenants have nowhere to self-serve.

No portal, no receipts on demand, no maintenance visibility. Every small request becomes another WhatsApp message.
