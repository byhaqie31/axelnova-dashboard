SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity) avoids fixing a valuation today. Investor commits capital now, equity is determined at next priced round at the lower of valuation cap or discount. Rolling close means commitments are accepted as they come, with no waiting for full round to clear. Investor benefits

What backers get beyond equity. Practical, deliverable, no fluff.

Monthly investor update

One-page email every month covering revenue, burn, customers, key learnings, and asks. First update sent within 30 days of close. Standard information rights.

02

Pro-rata rights for next round

Right to maintain ownership percentage in the next priced funding round. Protects against dilution as Roofly scales beyond seed.

03

Free Roofly Business tier for life

Lifetime access to the Business plan (RM 199/mo, unlimited units). Useful if investor owns or manages rental property. Converts backer into power user.

04

Advisory access · 30-min monthly call

Direct line to the founder for operational decisions, strategy questions, or product feedback. Optional, with no obligation to use it.
